Social Responsibility Initiatives

transcosmos Korea is actively carrying out social responsibility activities to enhance talent enhancement, environmental preservation, and the promotion of health.
To ensure a flourishing and convenient life for all, we continuously strive to improve society beyond corporate boundaries.

  • Sharing of ESG Information

    We actively share ESG information and conduct SNS certification events through our monthly organizational culture newsletters.

  • Book Donation Campaign

    transcosmos Korea's social responsibility activity project, 'Empty, Share, Fill' is a book donation campaign for the culturally underprivileged. By donating books, we envision a society where the local community grows alongside transcosmos Korea.

With Together

With our social responsibility program, 'With Together',
transcosmos Korea strives to provide a healthy and flourishing
society for our employees.

  • 01
    First Sponsorship Activity: "Let's Eat"

    Support children who need extra support during the winter vacation to give them a wholesome vacation by sponsoring their lunch.
    Sponsorship Amount: 20,000,000 KRW
    Beneficiaries: Lunch provided to 2,386 primary school students across 123 schools nationwide

  • 02
    Second Sponsorship Activity: "Empowering Females in Need"

    Support feminine hygiene products and COVID-19 prevention items for girls from families in crisis.
    Sponsorship Amount: 38,585,000 KRW
    Beneficiaries: Supply of products to 300 girls from disadvantaged households across the nation

  • 03
    Third Sponsorship Activity: "#Study Must Go On"

    Support IT learning devices for children experiencing difficulties with remote classes.
    Sponsorship Amount: 35,440,000 KRW
    Beneficiaries: Tablet PCs provided to 128 children nationwide, and webcams to 46 local child centers in Gunsan, Jeonbuk

  • 04
    Fourth Sponsorship Activity: "(part 1) Mental Wellness & (part 2) Healthy Lunchbox for Children

    Support the emotional stability and healthy growth of children.
    Sponsorship Amount: 30,000,000 KRW
    Beneficiaries: (part 1) Improving the environment of the psychological therapy center in Suwon, Gyeonggi-do.
    (part2) Support nutritious side dishes for 75 children in Seoul.

  • 05
    Fifth Sponsorship Activity: "Study Must Go On" Season 2

    Support for children's families in need of improving the educational environment, offering home refurbishments such as wallpapering and flooring installations along with providing furniture for study.
    Financial Contribution: 30,621,900 KRW
    Beneficiaries: Underprivileged children in need of residential environment improvement

  • 06
    Sixth Sponsorship Activity: "Support for Barrier-Free Children's Rights Education Contents"

    We extend support to facilitate barrier-free educational content focused on children's rights, specifically curated for children with auditory and visual impairments.
    Financial Contribution: 28,400,000 KRW
    Beneficiaries: 12 educational institutions nationwide catering to visually/aurally impaired children (Total 349 students)

Environmental Responsibility

TCK is conducting a campaign "for our Earth", which is an environmental protection program that can be easily practiced in the office.

  • 01
    Preserving Our Precious Earth

    With the global awakening towards environmental preservation and an escalating consumer inclination towards eco-friendly practices, transcosmos Korea has stepped up its game. We encourage mindful recycling of PET bottles through a targeted campaign themed 'Rinse with water, Remove the label'.

  • 02
    The One Paper Towel Campaign

    Considering the month-long decomposition period of a single paper towel and the alarming deforestation it leads to, transcosmos Korea has launched a campaign. TCK is implementing a campaign to reduce the excessive usage of paper towels, making a contribution to environmental protection.
