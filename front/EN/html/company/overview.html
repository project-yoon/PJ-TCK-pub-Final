transcosmos Korea of Innovation and Enterprising Spirit

transcosmos Korea is a Korean subsidiary of transcosmos Inc., a distinguished global provider of IT solutions and services. Since its establishment in May 2001, TCK has shown a relentless and persistent growth, rebranding from CICI Korea in August 2009. We currently offer leading-edge technology and services for Digital Transformation of our client companies across a myriad of industrial sectors, encompassing finance, public institutions, and telecommunications. We present an all-encompassing and comprehensive portfolio of IT services by leveraging state-of-the-art IT technologies, including business process optimization, system development and maintenance, cloud computing, AI, big data, and cybersecurity.

Capitalizing on our expansive global network, we provide robust support to diverse multinational enterprises in their global business endeavors. Our unwavering commitment to delivering customer-centric services enables us to offer best solutions that reflect the diverse needs of our clients. By collaborating with both domestic and international technical partners, we stay ahead of the curve by integrating emerging technologies and productizing them, thus retaining our competitive edge.
