As a leader in the global BPO marketplace, transcosmos serves a comprehensive range of services that enhance cost optimization of corporate business processes and revenue augmentation with our workforce of over 66,000 professionals, based in 168 locations across 30 countries, most primarily in Asia.
The expansion of transcosmos Group and transcosmos Korea into a corporate consortium with a presence in 168 global locations and over 66,000 employees, is attributable to our shared journey of growth alongside our client companies' businesses. We strive to consistently deliver a higher level of service that provides additional revenues and profits for our client companies' growth, extending beyond simple cost-saving BPO services.

Premium BPO services are based on an optimal symbiosis of people and technology and are only achievable when the satisfaction of client companies and employees is proportional.
Through understanding our clients' businesses, innovating new technologies, and investing in employees' training and growth, we strive to generate the greatest benefits for our client companies. transcosmos Korea Inc. Hiroyuki Tani, CEO
